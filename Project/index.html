About Us

Our Company is acting as an intermediary between the registered user and the advocates and is providing an online platform for the registered user to easily connect with the professional advocates. It provides an easy and user-friendly channel for the registered users to voice their grievances, and the company provides assistance to the registered user by coordinating with their panel advocates to provide consultancy services to the client and to initiate suitable legal action.

Our Key Features

Easy Complaint Filing

An easy-to-use online platform to register consumer complaints, seek legal advice, and get justice. Consumers can file a complaint from their mobile through this portal and send a legal notice to the company.
